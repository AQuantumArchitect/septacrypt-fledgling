## Core vision

"An AI powered emergent story universe sandbox" — procedural generation +
emergent narrative; an engine generating complete game content (models,
storylines, dialogue, mechanics). Influences: RayDream Designer's procedural
vases (1997), Dwarf Fortress (2006), Minecraft (2010); the Enterprise
schematic insight ("push buttons to auto-generate entire vessels").

## Core mechanics

- **Tutorial framework:** player IS a developing AI, progressing from simple
  machinery repairs to galactic interactions (→ septacrypt's SI framing and
  the Repair Station vertical slice).
- **Character creation:** worlds generated backward and forward in time,
  across scales, around a chosen character concept (→ retro-sim, ScaleAddress).
- **Subroutines & automation:** repeated tasks become automatable subroutines
  consuming processing power (→ umwelt agency loop, shadow-first promotion).
- **Time perception:** resource allocation affects perceived time; full CPU
  accelerates time but reduces reactivity (→ attention budget, FF/surprise).

## Facets (mono-aspect)

TranquiliTREEpose (procedural "magic"/growth), Lucid (generative dreamscape),
Glyph Guesser (sequence puzzles), Bread Winner (non-numeric economics:
None/Bit/Little/Some/Lot/Most/All), Anteciplace (space-time backward:
justify present states with plausible history).

## Facets (multi-aspect)

Uplift (multi-scale voxel epic, 16 nested scales, plant simulation),
Warmth (attention-generated metaphysical resource; dream worlds; Chill as
entropy), Gerbil Journey (SI progression: nine mastery levels to chrono-
jumps), To See the Sky (tower-defense city-builder with the pacifist
Seneschal), Twinworld (Fay/Zarth duality), Star Sage (shape-language),
Destination (procedural exploration puzzles), **Endless Knot** (medieval
simulator: braided Markov chains, kaironic time, order-chaos ×
authority-submission axes, the player as KairomIcon manipulating probability
weights — the direct ancestor of the Knot Ledger + Berry Tape design), and
**Jackal Star** (the culmination: hyperspatial Seven Spirits framework —
Blessing, Glory, Honor, Power, Thanksgiving, Wisdom, Might — fractal
parametric axes; contact with a Blight-like evil that corrupts attention and
hunts beauty).

## Design philosophy

Indirect control over micromanagement; "If a character perceives the object
of one of their hopes they will immediately begin to create and act on a
plan to achieve or avoid it" (2012). Modular/moddable; "delineated fractally
nested sets of generative systems"; games riding atop ML systems "built
without hands."

## Note

The seven spirit axes here (Blessing, Glory, Honor, Power, Thanksgiving,
Wisdom, Might) differ from septacrypt-core's SpiritVector fields (wisdom,
might, wealth, power, glory, honor, blessing): **Thanksgiving vs wealth**.
Flagged for the Spirit-frame reconciliation in the vertical slice.
